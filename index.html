About Me

Hello, my name is Lauren Ingram. I was born and raised in beautiful Western North Carolina. In North Carolina, I attended Mayland Community College where I obtained an Associate of Arts degree. It was during my completion of this degree that I found my passion for Web Development and working with computers.
In 2014, I moved to Central Florida. Between January 2017 and August 2018, I studied web development using .NET at American Public University. I will graduate on November 15th, 2018.

I am a full-time employee at Universal Orlando Resort. I have been with Universal since May of 2015. I have been an attraction’s attendant during this entire time. This has given me some of the most incredible experiences I have ever had. These experiences have made me grow as a person. As a person attending school and working full time, it was challenging.

When I am not working on academic and career advances, I love to take in all Central Florida has to offer. I spend plenty of my time taking in the wonderful theme parks that call Central Florida home. There are very few things that give me more joy than being able to have these experiences. When I am at home, I love trying new recipes, learning how to embroider and cross-stitch, as well as enjoying movies and books
